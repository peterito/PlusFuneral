What We Do

We take responsibility for arranging the details of the funeral, from the practical elements, such as coffin, hearse, limousines to the church or crematorium, along with newspaper announcements, flowers, reception, service sheets, music requirements and any other requests that make the funeral the personal tribute that it deserves to be. In the case of any death, the first practical step is to arrange the transfer of the deceased into our care and then await instructions. We provide advice and guidance for all aspects of the funeral service. Detailed discussions of the various options and the coordination of funeral requirements usually occur at our offices but, if preferred, we can visit you in your own home. We would discuss:

The necessary registration and administration requirements.

Options, availability and booking of the funeral venue and Officiant.

Planning the funeral transport Viewing and dressing of your loved one.

Choosing a coffin or casket.

Newspaper announcements and printed orders of service.

Funeral flowers and venue flowers.

Special options such as alternative transport, music options, and opportunities for personalisation of the ceremony.

Post-funeral reception and refreshments.

Costs, payment terms and guidance.
